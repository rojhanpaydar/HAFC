Our Story

  • Love, passion, hard work, determination, focus, and drive. This is the story of Home Appliances Food Co.
  • The name is an homage to the mom and pop shops around this great city. Whether your business is in slinging burgers or selling toasters. There is no replacement for good honest hard work.
  • Our ingredients are high quality and we process as little as possible in order to keep the food simple and good. Our meat and produce are sourced from a butcher shop and a fresh produce stand all within a two block radius. We believe that when you support the people around you, you build a community. And when a community is strong it feels like a home.
  • We are a family run food truck pumping out high quality food at a reasonable price. We’re throwing it back to a time when businesses relied on quality and craftsmanship. We put our heart and soul into every aspect of our business and we believe it will be evident in every bite.
